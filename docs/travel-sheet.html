Route

Day 1 | Osaka

Dotonbori, Shinsaibashi, Minami dinner.

Day 2 | Kyoto

Transfer to Kyoto, hotel check-in, Kiyomizu-dera, Ninenzaka, Yasaka Pagoda, Gion, Nanzen-ji.

Day 3 | Arashiyama / Fuji Transfer

Arashiyama, return to Osaka, Kaiyukan, Shinkansen toward Mishima, transfer to Fujikawaguchiko, onsen check-in.

Day 4 | Mt. Fuji to Tokyo

Chureito / Shimoyoshida, Lake Kawaguchiko, transfer to Shibuya, Tokyo hotel check-in.

Day 5 | West Tokyo

Shibuya Crossing, Shibuya food walk, Shibuya Sky.

Day 6 | East Tokyo

Tokyo Skytree, Tokyo Solamachi, Akihabara.

Day 7 | Central Tokyo

Imperial Palace, Shinjuku, bag pickup, airport transfer.

Stays

  • Osaka: private/local or no-cost first night unless switched in the budget model.
  • Kyoto: Shijo Karasuma area baseline.
  • Mt. Fuji: Kawaguchiko / Fujikawaguchiko area baseline.
  • Tokyo: Shibuya / Jinnan baseline for Days 4-6.

Transit

  • Osaka to Kyoto: local rail transfer.
  • Shin-Osaka to Fuji gateway: Shinkansen toward Mishima, then transfer toward Kawaguchiko.
  • Kawaguchiko to Tokyo: transfer to Shibuya.
  • Day 7: allow extra buffer for bag pickup and airport transfer.

Emergency Notes

  • Japan emergency police: 110.
  • Japan fire / ambulance: 119.
  • Keep embassy, airline, insurance, hotel phone numbers, and real booking references in a private offline copy.
  • If phone data fails, use station staff, hotel front desk, or a koban police box for route help.
